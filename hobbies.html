Landing Bio Hobbies

One of Justin’s favorite past times is board games. Here he is playing Settlers of Catan with his wife and her family. While they have since moved on to more complex titles, he still feels that Settlers of Catan is a great introduction to the hobby.

At every opportunity Justin and his wife Brandy try to travel the world. They prefer trips that force themselves out of their comfort zones be it an arduous four-day trek, volunteering at an Elephant shelter in Thailand, or as depicted here on a journey across Iceland.

When they can’t travel, Justin and Brandy enjoy participating in extreme sports events such as the annual Warrior Dash. Here they run a 10 mile course across over 25 obstacles of varying difficulty. In the end you’re awarded with mugs of beer.
